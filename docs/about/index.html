About

Warm, evidence-based therapy with depth and care

I help adults navigate anxiety, OCD, life transitions, and relationship patterns with practical tools and deeper understanding.

Hello, I’m Hailey Sayegh, a Licensed Professional Counselor based in Washington, DC. I work with adults seeking practical skills and deeper understanding to navigate anxiety, OCD, life transitions, self-esteem, and relationship patterns.

My style is warm, straightforward, and collaborative. I integrate psychodynamic therapy (to understand what’s underneath) with Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (to develop tools you can use day-to-day). For OCD and social anxiety, I incorporate Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP).

Therapy with me is an active process. We’ll focus on what matters most to you, set clear goals, and check in about what’s working so we can adjust as needed.

FAQs Therapy Approaches

Three aspects of my work

Mindfulness

Through mindfulness, we’ll open to the tender places within with kindness, curiosity, and non-judgment—so you can live with more meaning and freedom.

Psychodynamic

Therapy is a sacred space to explore who you are—bringing to light patterns and dynamics outside of awareness and turning insights into change.

Experiential

We’ll create a trusting space to decipher relationship patterns and practice new ways of relating in real time.

Work with me

My guiding principles

Root Work

We’ll name the systems we’re embedded in and explore intersecting identities, histories, and the stories we carry—so healing can take root.

Deshame

Together we’ll identify false narratives of “not good enough,” and come to know the truth of our worthiness.

Empower

We’ll honor the value in natural ways of being—deeply feeling, attuned, and creative—and cultivate gentle strength and community.
