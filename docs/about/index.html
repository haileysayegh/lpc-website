About Me

Warm, collaborative, and evidence-based care.

Hello, I’m Hailey Sayegh, a Licensed Professional Counselor based in Washington, DC. I work with adults seeking practical skills and deeper understanding to navigate anxiety, OCD, life transitions, self-esteem, and relationship patterns.

My style is warm, straightforward, and collaborative. I integrate psychodynamic therapy (to understand what’s underneath) with Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (to develop tools you can use day-to-day). For OCD and social anxiety, I incorporate Exposure and Response Prevention (ERP).

Therapy with me is an active process. We’ll focus on what matters most to you, set clear goals, and check in about what’s working so we can adjust as needed.
